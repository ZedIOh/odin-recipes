Quick and Easy Cereal

Description

Make your favourite bowl of cereal in less than 60 seconds, for a quick get up and go in the morning.

Ingredients

  • Your favourite Cereal
  • Your choice of Milk

Steps:

  1. Collect bowl, spoon, milk and box of cereal.
  2. Pour milk into bowl
  3. Add Cereal!
  4. Enjoy!!
